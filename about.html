I am a passionate creative with over 11 years of experience, including an MFA in Design from SVA.

Where I'm From

I was born and raised in a small town in Southern Utah. Some of my earliest memories include spending time in my Dad's custom cabinet shop, helping him with his work. I think seeing things built from scratch sparked my interest in creating things myself.

What was my start in design

Music originally brought me to design. I played in a band in highschool that needed posters and shirts printed to sell at our house shows. It was exciting to see people wear and use the things I had created. The design mediums have changed throughout the years, but the passion for design has stayed with me.

What I’ve Been up To

I’ve worked for corporations (Purple, Sunrun), small startups (Rumple, ApplicantPro), and freelanced (tons of great studios and companies). I also spent time in NYC earning a MFA from SVA (I miss NYC so much).

Currently I’m the UI/UX Manager at Breeze Airways, where the team I am building is creating the future of air travel.

What I’m looking for

Creative, impactful work with collaborative, driven teams. Also looking for more pickup basketball games.
