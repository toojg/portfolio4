Personal Project

Change Dixie Website

Design and Development
Launched 2020
Visit the website

I started this project to spread information about my undergrad school's name, “Dixie”, and its ties to the Antebellum South. The goal was to encourage Southern Utah residents to engage in the discussion around renaming Dixie State University. I was for the change.
‍
The website shows newspapers, yearbooks, and articles that connect the University to the Confederacy. I found that much of this information wasn't widely known, and invited the community to consider stepping away from these connections and consider change.

Intro video filmed with a drone on Dixie State University campus

On July 1, 2022, Dixie State University officially changed its name to Utah Tech University.
